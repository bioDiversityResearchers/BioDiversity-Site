About BioDiversity

Our purpose!

Since 2022, we have recognized the deep passion many under-represented communites have for biology. To represent and recognize these communites, we hope to shed light on the importance their curiosity and contributions are to society.

Our mission is to empower every lower-represented ethnicity as a way to bring equity into the workforce and recognize that everyone's hardwork is just as equal to whomever they may compare themselves too. Here, you will find work done by other youth researchers to inspire your ideas in your own research!
